About this project

A Computer Engineering mini-project from MIT Academy of Engineering, Pune

📋

Problem Statement

Climate change is accelerating due to everyday carbon emissions from transport, electricity, food and waste. Yet most people have no clear picture of their personal contribution. This tool bridges that gap by giving everyone an instant, personalised CO₂ analysis — and actionable steps to reduce it.
